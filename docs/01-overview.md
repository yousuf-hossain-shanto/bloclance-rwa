# Product Overview

## What is SurgeXRP

Web app for fractional ownership and trading of tokenized real estate. Each property is split into units; users buy units, earn rental yield (ROI), and can resell on a secondary marketplace using XRP or RLUSD.

Tagline (from Explore hero): *"Browse curated properties and invest in seconds. Every asset on SurgeXRP is selected for stable rental yield and consistent income."*

Marketing line (from unauth Overview): *"Supercharge Your Earnings With RWA — Earn passive incomes from RWA all over the globe and grow your portfolio."*

## Value proposition

- **Curated assets** — vetted real estate (residential, commercial, luxury) across global markets (US, EU, Asia).
- **Low ticket** — minimum investment **$1,000 (5 units)**.
- **Passive yield** — ROI shown per asset, ranges 2.5%–14.2% in current designs.
- **Liquidity** — secondary marketplace with market & limit orders, peer-to-peer.
- **XRPL-native** — settlement in XRP and RLUSD (Ripple stablecoin).

## Scope (from Figma)

Three main app sections, gated by auth:

1. **Explore** — public, browseable property catalog + asset detail.
2. **Marketplace** — auth-only secondary trading (buy/sell units).
3. **Overview** — auth-only portfolio dashboard, withdrawals, profile.
